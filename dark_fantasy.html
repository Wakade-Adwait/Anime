Death note

Death note is the manga that is recommendedd for beeginner watcher.The story revovles around Light Yagami a genius high school student who discovers a mysterious notebook: the "Death Note", which belonged to the Ryuk, a shinijami and grants the user the supernatural ability to kill anyone whose name is written in its pages. The series centers around Light's subsequent attempts to use the Death Note to carry out a worldwide massacre of individuals whom he deems immoral and to create a crime-free society, using the alias of a god-like vigilante named "Kira", and the subsequent efforts of an elite Japanese police task force, led by enigmatic detective L, to apprehend him. The series was released on 4th|October|2006 by Tsugumi Ohba

Jujutsu kaisen

Jujutsu Kaisen is the manga series written byGege Akutami.The story follows high school student Yuji Itadori as he joins a secret organization of Jujutsu Sorcerers to eliminate a powerful Curse named Ryomen Sukuna,of whom Yuji becomes the host.The story also had the powerful character like Gojo Satoru ,Fushiguro Megumi.The anime was released on3rd|Octobere|2020 .

Record of ragnarok

Record of ragnarok series is written by Shinya Umemura and Takumi Fukui and illustrated by Azychika about a fighting tournament featuring prominent historical figures against gods from various mythologies, with the fate of mankind in the balance. It began in Coamix's seinen manga magazine Monthly Comic Zenon in November 2017. It was licensed in North America by Viz Media in June 2021.
The manga was adapted as an original net animation (ONA) series by Graphinica and premiered in June 2021 on Netflix. A second season by Graphinica and Yumeta Company premiered in January 2023.The 1st Season was released on 17|june|2021

Hell's paradise

Hell's paradise written and illustrated by Yuji Kaku.it follows the ninja Gabimaru and the executioner Yamada Asaemon Sagiri as they search for the elixir of immortality. An anime television series adaptation produced by MAPPA aired from April to July 2023. A second season has been announced.The show was released on 01|APRIL|2023.

Chainsaw man

Chainsaw man is written and illustrated by Tatsuki Fujimoto .Chainsaw Man follows the story of Denji, an impoverished teenager who makes a contract that fuses his body with that of a dog-like Devil named Pochita, granting him the ability to transform parts of his body into chainsaws. Denji eventually joins the Public Safety Devil Hunters, a government agency focused on fighting against Devils whenever they become a threat to Japan.The show was released on 12|October|2022.
