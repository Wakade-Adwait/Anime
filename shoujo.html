Komi-san can't communicate

Komi-san is anime which has an introvert girl and extrovert boy .This story revolves around Komi and her friends who has difficulties in communicating with people.The author of this manga series is Tomohito Oda The anime was released on 7th|October|2021

Your name

'Your name' is an anime movie created byMakoto Shinkai.Your name A.K.A Kimi no na has the story of high school students Taki Tachibana and Mitsuha Miyamizu, who suddenly began to swap bodies despite having never met, unleashing chaos on each other's lives. The film was inspired by the frequency of natural disasters in Japan.The movie was released on 26th|August|2016 in Japan and in 2017 internationally.

Horimiya is story about Hori-san and Miyamura-kun byHiroki Adachi.This story is basically about a highschool couples .Miyamura who was a isolateed kid in his class was in relationship with Hori-san in their IIIrd year of highschool.The series was released on 18th|July|2023.


Danger in my heart

Danger in my heart is story about Kyotaro a middle class boy who loves reading books in library where he and Anna first met. Initially, Kyotaro had delusions of killing Anna and making her corpse his alone, but they're replaced by romantic affection as he spends more time around her. the anime was created by Sakuri Norio and releaseed on 1st|April|2023.
